About Us

The initial concept of Talent Tree and it’s ground work began in 2014. After a series of tests, trails, freelance works and market research, Talent Tree Recruitment began the 1st phase of operations in 2016 in Melbourne. It’s 2nd phase of operation will launch in 2017 across Australia.

Talent Tree’s core founders came from IT and Business Anthropology. Thus, we take a different approach to the recruitment process. We are proud of our structured and unique talent acquisition process that ensures right fit candidates for employers to be matched in a timely and cost effective way. We have been networking and collecting our candidate contacts over the past 5 years. Also, we continue to grow our reach to talented professional.

Our core values

  • As a caring and client focused agency, we actively listen to our clients or candidates and seek to find best possible solution for them.
  • Integrity is our backbone. It is the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles when conducting our affairs.
  • We incorporate fun based activities to fulfil objectives in our organisation. Rather than the structured 9-5 working hours at office we tend to tailor it per individual needs.
  • We welcome diversity and equality. We tend to avoid typical hierarchy management structures rather we treat people based on their talent regardless race or background.

Range of Services

Talent Tree can manage volumes from individual assignments, through to project and high volume recruitment. Incorporating permanent, temporary and fixed contract, Talent Tree provides retained and contingent recruitment services to Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Perth and Darwin markets.
